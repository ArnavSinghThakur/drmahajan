About the Doctor

A Decade of
Expert Care

Dr. Ramit Mahajan is Professor of Gastroenterology and Hepatology with over a decade of clinical expertise and research experience. He has made significant contributions through his work in inflammatory bowel disease, fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT), and liver failure management, with research published in the New England Journal of Medicine — receiving over 815 citations.

His clinical expertise spans the full range of gastroenterological conditions — from complex IBD cases needing advanced biologics, to critical liver failure managed with plasmapheresis.

Whether handling functional disorders, advanced endoscopy, or acute liver failure, Dr. Mahajan ensures each patient receives individualized care grounded in the latest evidence.

Combining evidence-based care with personalized treatment strategies — so every patient receives individualized attention rooted in the latest science.
